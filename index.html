Raquel Forés Toribio

Postdoctoral researcher at The Ohio State University

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Astronomy and CCAPP Fellow since October 2024. My research interests are Time-Domain Astronomy and Gravitational Lensing. I defended my PhD thesis entitled "Gravitationally lensed quasars: from stars to galaxy clusters" in September 2024 at the Universitat de València, after earning a Master’s Degree in Astrophysics from the Universidad de La Laguna in 2020 and a Bachelor Degree in Physics from the Universitat de València in 2018.
